About Me

Dedicated to environmental journalism and documentary production, focusing on critical issues affecting our planet.

As the Chief of Environment & Climate Change Desk at Channel I Bangladesh, I cover crucial environmental topics including river pollution, erosion, climate migration, biodiversity conservation, and wildlife protection. My work extends to documenting the lives of tigers, Nilgai, vultures, elephants, and turtles.

I'm also a voice-over artist and documentary producer, creating compelling narratives about environmental challenges. My recent research paper "Status of Environment in Gazipur" (2024) and various awards, including Best Reporter (Environment 2023) from America Bangladesh Press Club, reflect my commitment to environmental journalism.
